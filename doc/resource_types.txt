Resource types
==============

Internal resource types
-----------------------
### Metal ores
All of the below listed are resources that are transformed into an equivalent
mass of metal once refined.
  * ore_iron
  * ore_copper
  * ore_tungsten
  * ore_platinum
  * ore_uranium

Unrefined ores are not eligible for export.


### Metals
Refined metals may be either exported or used in further construction.


### Food
Food is a special resource, in that only so much can be stored as there are
freezers. Food may not be exported.
